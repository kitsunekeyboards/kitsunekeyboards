About the Shapeshifter

Easy to use

The Kitsune Shapeshifter is flexible and easy to use; it comes presoldered and assembled so you can just plug it in and get to typing! It also comes with hot swap sockets so you can put in the switches that YOU love!

Compatible & affordable

The Kitsune Shapeshifter is designed to be compatible with most standard tray mount 60% cases - making it more affordable because you don't need to buy another specialized case to work with this keyboard.

Practical Ergonomics

Take the Kitsune Shapeshifter with you wherever you go! Its portable design means you can set it on top of your laptop keyboard and stop juggling all your keyboard gear from your desk to your next meeting.
